🧘 Safe Medical Communication Rules

🩺

Doctor First

Lab results should always be validated by a physician. A single out-of-range value does not necessarily mean you have a disease.

🧪

Reference Ranges

Normal reference ranges vary between different testing labs. Always compare your specific numbers with the range listed on the paper.

⏱️

Fasting Status

Some panels (lipid profile, blood sugar) require fasting for 8-12 hours beforehand. Keep this in mind when discussing results.

🚨

Urgent Findings

If a value is flagged as critically high or low, show it to your primary healthcare provider immediately. Call 112 for emergencies.

Source: ICMR Guidelines, WHO References & Standard Medical Resources | Not a substitute for professional medical advice
