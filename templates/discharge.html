🧘 Basic Guidelines for Safe Recovery

🚶

Activity & Rest

Perform light walking around the room to prevent blood clots. Avoid lifting heavy weights or driving until cleared.

💧

Diet & Hydration

Stick to simple, soft home meals. Avoid oily, spicy food. Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily to support healing.

💊

Medications

Always complete full antibiotic cycles even if feeling better. Set reminders to avoid skipping critical doses.

🚨

Danger Symptoms

Watch for red flags: high fever, severe unmanageable pain, heavy bleeding, or breathing trouble. Call 112 immediately.
